## [0.2.0][] - 2025-09-10

Version 0.2 improves support for esbuild watch mode.

[0.2.0]: https://github.com/zombiezen/esbuild-plugin-stimulus/releases/tag/v0.2.0

### Added

- Trigger rebuilds in watch mode on changes in appropriate directories
